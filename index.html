How to Compress Photo and Add Date Online for Govt Exams?

Filling out online forms for government exams like SSC CGL, UPSC, Railway (RRB), and State PSCs requires strict adherence to photo guidelines. If your passport size photo is over 50KB or missing the Date of Photo (DOP), your application might get rejected. Here is a step-by-step guide to using our free photo compressor:

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Click on the upload section above and select your raw photograph or signature. Our tool automatically converts HEIC or PNG files into the widely accepted JPG/JPEG format.

Step 2: Add Name & Date

Type your full name and select the current date from the calendar. The tool will automatically generate a perfectly aligned white strip at the bottom of your photo as per SSC/UPSC notifications.

Step 3: Adjust Size to 20KB or 50KB

Use our smart quality slider. Watch the "Live Output Size" counter. For signatures, keep it under 20KB. For standard passport photos, keep it under 50KB.

Step 4: Download Securely

Click "Compile Photograph" and download your file. All processing is done strictly within your browser (client-side), meaning your private documents are never uploaded to any external server.
