How to Add Date in Passport Size Photo?

Official government exam portals like SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS) and UPSC often require candidates to have their Date of Photo (DOP) printed on their passport photographs. Here is how you can use this tool to stay compliant:

  • Step 1: Upload your passport size photo (JPG or PNG).
  • Step 2: Enter your name if required, or leave it blank to add only date in passport size photo.
  • Step 3: Select the Date of Photo from the calendar.
  • Step 4: Adjust the quality slider to keep the file size between 20KB and 50KB.
  • Step 5: Click Generate and Download your form-ready photo.
